Our Vision

John Lewis was a civil rights activist who famously said to
"Speak up, speak out, get in the way. Get in good trouble, necessary trouble, and help redeem the soul of America."

We strive to live up to this ideal as we peacefully fight threats to democracy and human rights abuses in America. We aim to be the spark that ignites a revolution of freedom and human decency.
